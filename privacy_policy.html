🚀 Privacy Policy

Space Explorer 3D – Galactic Math Tour

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Summary: Space Explorer 3D does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. This app is designed to be completely safe for children.

1. Who We Are

Space Explorer 3D is an educational multiplication game for children aged 5–10, developed by Robert Amador (Sparky78606). If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: sparky78606@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever.

Space Explorer 3D does NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Any information from children or adults

3. Internet Access

The app requests internet permission in order to support Google Play's billing infrastructure. The app itself does not make any network requests, does not communicate with any servers, and does not transmit any data over the internet. All game content — questions, space facts, animations — runs entirely on the device.

4. Third-Party Services

The only third-party integration in this app is Google Play Billing, which handles the one-time app purchase. We do not have access to any payment information — all transactions are processed entirely by Google. Google's privacy policy applies to that transaction and can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics platforms, crash reporting tools, or any other third-party SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Space Explorer 3D is designed for children aged 5–10. We take children's privacy extremely seriously and fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Because we collect no data at all:

  • No parental consent is required
  • There is nothing to delete or request
  • Children cannot accidentally share personal information
  • There are no accounts, profiles, or user identities of any kind

6. Data Storage

The app does not store any user data on our servers because we have no servers. The app may store basic game preferences locally on the device using Android's standard local storage — this data never leaves the device and is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

7. Advertising

Space Explorer 3D contains no advertisements of any kind. There are no banner ads, video ads, interstitial ads, or any advertising SDKs integrated into the app.

8. In-App Purchases

This app is a paid app available for a one-time purchase of $0.99 on the Google Play Store. There are no additional in-app purchases, subscriptions, or microtransactions within the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we collect no data, any future changes would only occur if we add new features that require data handling, at which point we would update this policy accordingly and notify users through the app store listing.
